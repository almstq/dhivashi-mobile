Agentic-First Stack

\- Frontend: Flutter (mobile), Next.js (web).

\- Backend: FastAPI/LangGraph agents.

\- DB: Postgres/PostGIS/pgvector (Supabase).

\- Auth: Clerk (+960/WhatsApp).

\- Maps: Mapbox + OSRM.

\- Observability: LangSmith.

Principles: Audit every action, offline-first, resilient.
